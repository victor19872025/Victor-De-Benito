About

Améd Victor Benito brings a unique blend of craftsmanship, innovation, and teaching to the world of spirits. Having led the creation of award-winning products such as DOJA, Nomad Oscuro, and Portal Gin, his journey began with production experience at London’s Thames Distillers and evolved into heading distillation for DOJA, an Indo-Japanese New Wave Craft Gin.

Now working independently, he supports brands and entrepreneurs who want to bring exceptional spirits to market—combining technical expertise with brand identity and market positioning insight.
